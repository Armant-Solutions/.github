## About

Armant was founded by a team of aerospace engineers with backgrounds spanning software development, aerospace design, high-tech R&D, government contracts, and commercial programs. Our principal team members are drawn from across the aerospace industry and bring decades of ground test, flight test, and multi-physics simulation experience.

Our goal is not just to hand customers data. It is to deliver the right solution, one that keeps working for them well into the future. We help teams right-size the problem, then apply the appropriate level of fidelity to answer the questions that actually drive the design.

From low-speed ducted flow to cutting-edge hypersonics, our work covers the breadth and depth of flight sciences design.

## What We Do

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Modeling and Simulation** | Multi-physics engineering simulation is our foundation. We have hands-on experience simulating challenging phenomena and extreme environments, and we know how to scope a simulation to answer the right question. |
| **CFD and Aerothermal Analysis** | Thermal and fluid analysis, from turbulent flow over a wall, to a rocket motor at start-up, to a vehicle at the edge of space. |
| **Test and Verification** | Decades of experience designing and executing ground test campaigns, including subsonic, supersonic, and hypersonic wind tunnel testing and arc jet testing, supporting designs through flight test and production. |
| **Data Analysis and Verification** | Analysis, research, and independent verification of engineering data, regardless of source. |
| **Design Analysis and Optimization** | Uncertainty quantification and multidisciplinary design analysis and optimization (MDAO), applied from the component level to the system level. |
| **Workflow Automation** | We believe the core of design engineering is decision-making. We automate and connect toolchains to trim busywork from the engineering workflow so engineers can make better-informed decisions on higher-quality data. |
| **Electronics and Power Systems Design** | Design, integration, and analysis of flight electronics, including power systems, flight controls, GPS/INS integration, avionics, and RF. |
